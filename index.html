Outstanding Core Features of The Product

It’s our largest display yet.

Big screen. Huge impact. The challenge was to create a bigger display while barely expanding the dimensions of the watch itself. To do so, the display was completely re‑engineered reducing the borders by 40%, allowing for more screen area than both Series 6 and Series 3.

Tap. Type. Swipe.

Across the system, buttons have been redesigned to take advantage of the larger display.So using apps like Calculator, Stopwatch, and Timer is easier than ever.An Apple Watch first — a QWERTY keyboard lets you tap or slide from letter to letter with QuickPath.It uses machine learning to predict the word you’re typing.

Always-On Retina display. A brighter view of you.

You don’t need to raise your wrist or touch the screen to see the time or other information and complications on your watch face, because the display is always on. And it’s 70% brighter indoors when your wrist is down.

Series 7 is the most durable Apple Watch ever built.

Fundamental design changes were needed to achieve the vision of the larger Always-On Retina display. These same innovations also helped make the most crack‑resistant front crystal yet.
